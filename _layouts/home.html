About the Project

The Future Programmers of Ukraine is a series of mini camps introducing Ukrainian youth (ages 10-16) to technology skills which will benefit their future education and expand their career prospects. Ukraine has a well-established IT industry projected to grow and demand more skilled labor in the foreseeable future. This project aims to prepare students for this reality and help them explore their potential.

What Students Will Learn

code

Programming

Using the Scratch programming language, students will build their own Android/iOS applications and computer games.

android

Robotics

Students will program mBot robots to perform different tasks like solving mazes, sumo wrestling, or playing football.

flash_on

Electronics

Basic electronics will be taught with the use of Raspberry Pi's, LED lights, and electronic sensors.
